Lift Log

Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Lift Log is developed and maintained by Anthony M. Dillof, an individual developer based in Michigan, USA. This privacy policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Lift Log does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All workout data you enter — including routines, exercises, sets, reps, and notes — is stored exclusively on your device. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to the developer or any third party.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in storage. It is not uploaded to any server. The developer has no access to your workout data at any time.

Feedback Email

If you choose to send feedback via the in-app feedback button, your email and any information you include will be received at lift.log.feedback@gmail.com. This information is used solely to improve the app and is never shared with third parties.

Third Party Services

Lift Log uses no third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services of any kind. There are no ads in this app.

Children's Privacy

Lift Log is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page and in the app's Play Store listing. Continued use of the app following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact: lift.log.feedback@gmail.com
